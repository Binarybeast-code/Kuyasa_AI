Learn what is CryptoCurrency??

Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual form of currency that uses cryptography for security and operates on decentralized blockchain technology, enabling secure, peer-to-peer transactions and asset ownership verification.

What is Physical Assets

Physical assets are tangible, real-world items with intrinsic value, such as real estate, machinery, vehicles, and inventory, used for production, investment, or personal use. They contrast with digital or financial assets.
